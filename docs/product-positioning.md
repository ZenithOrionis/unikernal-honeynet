# Product Positioning

## Who it is for

Enterprise Honeynet V2 is designed for mid-market SOC teams that need earlier signal on internet-facing reconnaissance, credential abuse, and distributed probing but do not want a hosted control plane or a heavy deception program.

## Problem it solves

Most teams only see scanning and credential abuse when it reaches perimeter devices, WAF logs, or production applications. This platform moves that signal forward in time by exposing deception sensors first and converting interactions into detections, investigations, and containment artifacts.

## Value proposition

- Earlier warning than production systems
- Clear containment guidance for lean analyst teams
- Self-hosted trust boundary for security-conscious organizations
- Fast evidence handoff to IR, SecOps, or management

## What it is not

- Not a SaaS control plane
- Not a full SOAR platform
- Not a full case-management suite
- Not a deep attacker emulation environment

## Core buyer message

The product is a deception-driven early-warning layer for internet-facing reconnaissance and credential abuse. It helps analysts decide what to block, what to investigate, and what to hand off.
